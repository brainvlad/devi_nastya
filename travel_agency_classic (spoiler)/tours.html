LONDON, Britain

Information

Breakfast. Moving to Scotland! The capital of this region of Great Britain is Edinburgh. Sightseeing tour of one of the most beautiful cities in Europe: Royal Mile, St. Giles Cathedral, John Knox House, Greyfriars Church, Parliament, Royal Palace, Holy Rud Palace and Abbey.
We offer those who wish to visit the South Scotland excursion (35 euros + entrance ticket).
Upon arrival, a city tour: Cologne Cathedral, Frew Beer House, Roncoli Square, Cologne Dwarfs Fountain, Old Market Square. Free time. Transfer to a hotel on the border of Germany and Belgium. Overnight.
